Prepared for: Modern Changes USA | Date: July 11, 2025

Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a multi-platform digital advertising campaign for Modern Changes USA to address challenges in brand awareness, online presence, and lead generation. We will implement a data-driven strategy across Google, Facebook, and Instagram to dominate local search, build an aspirational brand, and create a seamless customer journey. Our goal is to rapidly increase visibility, drive qualified traffic, and generate measurable growth in the DFW and Houston markets, transforming Modern Changes USA into a premium, accessible lifestyle brand.

A Full-Funnel Advertising Strategy

An integrated approach across Google, Facebook, and Instagram to guide customers from awareness to purchase.

Google Ads

Capture high-intent customers actively searching for your products and drive traffic to your stores.

  • 1
    Search Campaigns: Target keywords like "outdoor furniture Dallas" and "in-stock patio sets" to catch buyers at the perfect moment.
  • 2
    Performance Max: Leverage AI to reach customers across all Google channels (YouTube, Display, Maps) to maximize sales and store visits.
  • 3
    Local Campaigns: Drive foot traffic to your Houston and DFW locations by targeting users in a specific radius with ads on Google Maps.

Facebook & Instagram

Build a desirable brand, showcase product style, and nurture potential customers through visual storytelling.

  • 1
    Awareness (Top Funnel): High-quality video ads to tell your brand story and build an emotional connection.
  • 2
    Consideration (Mid Funnel): Carousel and Collection ads to showcase products. Lead ads to capture contact info with valuable offers.
  • 3
    Conversion (Bottom Funnel): Dynamic Product Ads to retarget website visitors and cart abandoners, driving them back to purchase.
